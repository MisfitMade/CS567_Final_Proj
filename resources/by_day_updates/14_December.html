Other dates in RuneScape

14 December is a date in the Gregorian calendar. In Gielinor, the date is equivalent to 19 Wintumber.

  • 2017 – Website update: King of the Skill Winners
  • 2016 – Game update: Deadman Season 4 Changes
  • 2016 – Game update: Carol's Christmas
  • 2003 – Game update: RS2 bugfixes - part 5
