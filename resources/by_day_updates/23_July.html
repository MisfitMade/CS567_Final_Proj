Other dates in RuneScape

23 July is a date in the Gregorian calendar. In Gielinor, the date is equivalent to 23 Fentuary.

  • 2020 – Game update: Poll 71 and Ferox Enclave Updates
  • 2015 – Game update: Big Cats & WWF
  • 2014 – Website update: F2P Trial Expansion
  • 2002 – Game update: Members' quests
