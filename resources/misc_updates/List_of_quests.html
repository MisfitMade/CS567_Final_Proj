Miniquests

Miniquests are groups of objectives that, unlike Quests, have no icon on the world map and offer no Quest points upon completion. There will also be no indication of starting nor completing a miniquest, other than what is written in the corresponding Quest List journal entry. There is a total of sixteen miniquests, which are all members-only.
