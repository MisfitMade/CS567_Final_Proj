(u) is a suffix usually denoting an item as unstrung, with a few exceptions.

  • shortbow (u)

  • Oak shortbow (u)

  • Willow shortbow (u)

Crossbows[edit | edit source]

  • Bronze crossbow (u)
  • Blurite crossbow (u)
  • Iron crossbow (u)
  • Steel crossbow (u)
  • Mithril crossbow (u)
  • Adamant crossbow (u)
  • Rune crossbow (u)
  • Dragon crossbow (u)

Exceptions[edit | edit source]

  • Iban's staff (u), referring to upgraded
  • Craw's bow (u), Viggora's chainmace (u), and Thammaron's sceptre (u) referring to uncharged
  • Chocolate mixture(u), referring to unfinished
