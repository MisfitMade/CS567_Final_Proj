Hidden updates are updates not announced or mentioned in the Old School RuneScape news or Patch Notes as they might be considered too trivial. These updates are usually minor changes that have no major impact on the game, but they may occasionally be notable and omitted for other reasons.

Below is a list of all known hidden updates.

2021[edit | edit source]

August[edit | edit source]

  • 20 August
    • An NPC in Prifddinas named Ian has removed.

2020[edit | edit source]

April[edit | edit source]

  • 10 April
    • The tracks Arachnids of Vampyrium, Predator Xarpus, and Dance of the Nylocas were all reworked.

2019[edit | edit source]

February[edit | edit source]

  • 13 February
    • A small fishing net spawn has been added to a ruined house east of the docks in Morytania.

2018[edit | edit source]

April[edit | edit source]

  • 19 April
    • Jeremy Servil from the Fight Arena quest has been renamed to Sammy Servil due to the similarity to disgraced British celebrity Jimmy Saville, who the character's name was a word play on.

2017[edit | edit source]

October[edit | edit source]

  • 14 October
    • The masked stranger has been removed.
  • 13 October
    • A masked stranger has been added to the game.

August[edit | edit source]

  • 10 August
    • Ugthanki kebabs have been made darker to distinguish it from regular kebabs.

2015[edit | edit source]

September[edit | edit source]

  • 10 September
    • Players who do not interact with the client would stop retaliating in combat after 15 minutes, making splashing a much less effective idle training method.

2014[edit | edit source]

June[edit | edit source]

  • 12 June
    • Players no longer need a chart, watch and sextant in their inventory to complete a coordinate clue.

May[edit | edit source]

  • 8 May
    • Erin has been removed.
  • 1 May
    • A duck named Erin has been added.
