General advice[edit | edit source]

  • Overloads will not let you drink a dose if you have less than 51 hitpoints.
    • If you use a locator orb or dwarven rock cake at too low of Hitpoints while the overload still has damage left to deal, you will die.
  • Absorption potions are most efficiently used with 1 Hitpoints accomplished with a dwarven rock cake or locator orb. Having 1 Hitpoints means any damage taken has a max hit of 1, at most draining a single absorption point. 2 Hitpoints means it is possible to take a max hit of 2, draining the points faster.
    • To remain at 1 Hitpoints, flick Rapid Heal on and then off about every 50 seconds.
    • Combining this benefit with Dharok the Wretched's equipment is a good training method for those with higher than 92 Hitpoints, otherwise the Obsidian armour set, berserker necklace, and Toktz-xil-ak or strength bonus equipment are better.
    • Use the dwarven rock cake right-click option Guzzle to get to 1 Hitpoints, the eat option only brings Hitpoints down to 2.
  • Leaving the Nightmare Zone arena resets all stats.
  • In rumble mode:
    • Completing all quests will reduce costs by 10,000 per run.
    • More bosses enabled rewards a higher percentage of their total points per kill. Disabling bosses that are unlocked from completing quests reduces points per kill.
    • Some bosses are recommended to almost always be disabled based on their mechanics: Glod, Elvarg, Corrupt Lizardman, Chronozon, and Barrelchest.
    • Disabling a boss also disables every boss from that quest, e.g. disabling Damis also disables Dessous, Fareed, Kamil. Because of this mechanic all bosses from Dream Mentor, Recipe for Disaster, and Desert Treasure should always be enabled when trying to maximise points per hour.
    • Some bosses provide increased experience per damage dealt: Moss Guardian (+7.5%; 4.3xp), Dad (+10%; 4.4xp), Ice Troll King (+12.5%; 4.5xp), and Arrg (+17.5%; 4.7xp).
    • Avoid using Ultimate Force power-ups unless there are many bosses that you cannot damage.
    • Activate all other power-ups. With Power Surge, use a special attack weapon such as dragon claws or granite maul with an ornate handle. Damage dealt by Zapper or Recurrent Damage does not yield experience, but they do not usually impact experience rates unless the player kills bosses too quickly.

Getting your first points[edit | edit source]

If it is your first time playing Nightmare Zone, you will not have any points for absorptions or overloads. Follow either the Melee (Prayer) setup below with the recommended bosses or use hard endurance mode, killing every boss up to Elvarg. If equipped, you can attempt to go further, but it may be less efficient.

Playing on normal difficulty is also an option, however the amount of time and money it takes to earn a sustainable amount of points will increase as well as some bosses like Ice Troll King and Jungle Demon using more attack styles than just melee making it harder to survive.

For hard endurance dreams, the following bosses require special mechanics or extra items brought with you, making it far less AFK:

  • Corrupt Lizardman - Poison protection
  • Elvarg - Anti-dragon shield equipped - provided
  • Slagilith - Optional: Pickaxe - provided
  • Dagannoth mother - Only takes one type of damage in one form at a time
  • Dad - Stuns and knocks back
  • Tanglefoot - Equip Magic secateurs to deal damage - provided
  • Headless Beast - Drops rock from the ceiling when in melee range
  • Glod - Disables and drains Prayer, causes you to run towards or away from him
  • Flambeed - Equip ice gloves to deal damage
  • Gelatinnoth Mother - Only takes one type of damage in one form at a time
  • Karamel - Drains stats and Prayer, interrupts auto-retaliate and movement
  • Chronozon - Can only be killed after using all four blast spells
  • Giant Roc - Stuns and knocks back
  • Damis - Second form drains Prayer quickly.
  • Fareed - Equip ice gloves to deal damage
  • Kamil - Interrupts movement
  • Barrelchest - Deals high damage, drains Prayer and Defence, turns off protection prayers before attacking.
  • Elven traitor - High damage and prayer disabling arrows
  • Essyllt - Long ranged attacks, stat drain and stuns

Experience and points balance[edit | edit source]

Ironmen looking to imbue a slayer helmet or berserker ring can use a setup that balances points and experience to conserve Prayer potions and compensate for restricted access to Dharok the Wretched's equipment or Obsidian armour. Use the Optimal experience setup - #Melee (Absorption, AFK) method below, but use customisable hard rumble and the same bosses.

This yields approximately 250,000 points per hour and melee experience of between 50,000 and 60,000 per hour at 65 and higher attack, strength, and defence, and 80,000 per hour at 75 and higher, continuing to scale with higher combat levels.

Optimal experience setups[edit | edit source]

This setup uses a mix of strength and prayer bonus and is a fairly AFK strategy for experience and Nightmare Zone points. Players can gain up to around 90,000 melee experience and 400,000 Nightmare Zone points per hour. This requires prayer potions, somewhat expensive and worse overall for melee training, but does not require expensive gear and is more AFK.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Activate Protect from Melee and boost. When using divine super combat potions, maintain Hitpoints by activating Rapid Heal.

Damage boosting prayers such as Piety or Ultimate Strength can be used to increase experience and points per hour, but it increases prayer drain, requiring more attention and prayer potions.

Do not activate Ultimate Force. Activate Zapper and Recurrent Damage to increase points per hour, this may slightly affect experience gained. Activate Power Surge, use your special attack weapon, it lasts 45 seconds.

Dharok the Wretched's equipment with absorptions requires more attention than the other setups, but offers the fastest Attack and Strength experience of up to 180,000 experience per hour using dragon claws for special attacks during Power Surge, or up to 155,000 with a granite maul with an ornate handle. It is recommended to only use the setup with 92 Hitpoints or greater because of the set effect increasing damage when missing more HP. Switches can be brought for strength bonus during Power Surge. The rest of the equipment maximises strength and accuracy bonuses. It is possible to sustain around 1 hour per dream with this setup.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Run to any corner except the south-east corner, drink all your absorptions and sip a dose of overload. Feel the Locator orb or guzzle the dwarven rock cake down to 1 Hitpoints. Quickly turn on and off Rapid Heal to reset Hitpoints regeneration. Turn on Piety and attack or use auto-retaliate.

Drink prayer potions as needed, flick Rapid Heal on and off once every 50-60 seconds to avoid regenerating to 2 Hitpoints. This maximises damage dealt and minimises absorption points used. If your Hitpoints goes above 1, feel the locator orb or guzzle the dwarven rock cake, if it is not working run away temporarily. As the overload expires, either run or turn on protection prayers temporarily to avoid wasting absorption points, drink another dose of overload and continue as before.

Experienced prayer flickers can set up quick-prayers to simultaneously activate Rapid Heal and their best offensive prayer. This improves damage output, stops accidental regeneration, and maximises Prayer points without using prayer potions, but this requires significantly more effort and attention.

Do not activate Ultimate Force. Activate Zapper and Recurrent Damage to increase points per hour, this may slightly affect experience gained only if you normally kill the bosses before new ones take their place. Activate Power Surge, switch equipment and use your special attack weapon, it lasts 45 seconds.

Using normal customisable rumble with absorptions allows for 20 minutes of AFK melee training. After 20 minutes, the player will stop attacking and must manually start attacking again to reset the timer. Players can gain up to 85,000â€“90,000 experience per hour, and sustain for 3â€“6 hours per session depending on equipment and stats. While this method does earn slightly more Nightmare Zone points than spent on absorptions, players looking to solely gain points are advised to use other setups. It is possible to use this setup on customisable hard mode; however, bosses hit much harder in the hard mode, requiring more absorption potions. This does net more points (250k/hr+) and is a good mix of points and experience, especially for Ironmen looking to imbue items. Players looking to gain more experience per hour are advised to use the Dharok's setup for much faster experience.

Players who want sustainability but cannot afford a ghrazi rapier can use an abyssal whip (for training Attack or Defence) or abyssal bludgeon (for training Strength) along with Bandos armour.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Run to the middle of the arena. Use absorptions until 951-1000 absorption points, and optionally feel a locator orb or guzzle a dwarven rock cake down to 1 Hitpoints then sip a super combat potion. Repeat every 15â€“20 minutes.

Using a locator orb or a dwarven rock cake is optional but allows for longer sessions, minimising the amount of absorption points used to tank damage (especially using obsidian armour).

Using a customisable normal rumble with absorptions maximises AFK time and allows for 20 minutes of AFK ranged training. After 20 minutes the player will stop attacking and must interact with the client to start attacking again. Players can gain up to 90,000â€“95,000 experience per hour when using a toxic blowpipe, or 70,000 experience with a magic shortbow (i). It is possible to sustain 3â€“5 hours per session depending on equipment and stats. While this method does earn slightly more Nightmare Zone points than spent on absorptions, players looking to gain points are advised to use other setups. It is possible to use this setup on customisable hard mode, but only recommended if not being as AFK. Bosses hit a lot harder in the hard mode, so it is much more important to flick Rapid Heal and use a locator orb or dwarven rock cake.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

During the 25 second countdown before bosses start spawning, boost with an overload, consume 1000 points of absorption points, and feel the Locator orb or guzzle the dwarven rock cake down to 1 Hitpoints. Quickly flick Rapid Heal on and then off to reset the Hitpoints regeneration timer and do so every 50-60 seconds to maintain 1 Hitpoints. If your Hitpoints ever regen to 2, use the Locator orb or rock cake again to get back to 1 Hitpoint.

When your overload is about to run out, make sure you have 200+ points of Absorption remaining, and as soon as it runs out use another dose. Try to avoid being attacked as much as possible while the overload reduces you from 51 to 1 Hitpoints, to prevent wastage of Absorption points. Follow the boss kill priority list provided to maximise points gained.

Do not use Ultimate Force power-up unless there are too many bosses that cannot be killed easily. Always use the Power Surge power-up and use the special attack weapon constantly. Use Recurrent Damage and Zapper whenever for higher points per hour.

A prayer potion should be used after suffering prayer-draining attacks from Karamel to ensure you can still flick Rapid Heal or activate Protect from Melee when your overload expires. Protect from Magic can be used against Karamel to minimise her stat drains.

Using the best Ranged equipment is optimal, but some bosses have very high Ranged defence, so it is recommended to bring a Magic switch. Elite Void Knight equipment or Void Knight equipment is not recommended, however they can be used if lacking Ranged equipment, especially for ironmen. 61 Magic is the minimum requirement to be able to cast Fire Wave with an overload potion.

Inventory[edit | edit source]

  • 4 overloads
  • 20 absorption potions
  • Special attack weapon for Power Surge
  • Dwarven rock cake or Locator orb
  • Ice gloves for Fareed and Flambeed
  • 1 Prayer potion for emergency
  • Optional: Magic switch for auto-casting spells

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Boost with an overload, consume 1000 points of absorption points, and feel the Locator orb or guzzle the dwarven rock cake down to 1 Hitpoints. Quickly turn on and off Rapid Heal to reset Hitpoints regeneration and do so every 50-60 seconds to maintain 1 Hitpoints.

Follow the boss kill priority list provided to maximise points gained.

Target the following bosses with Magic if you brought a switch:

  • Black Knight Titan
  • Ice Troll King
  • Chronozon - Attack with all four blast spells

Do not use Ultimate Force power-up unless there are too many bosses to deal with. Always use the Power Surge power-up and use the special attack weapon constantly. Use Recurrent Damage and Zapper whenever possible.

A Prayer potion should be used after suffering prayer-draining attacks from Karamel to ensure you can still flick rapid heal or activate protect from melee when your overload expires. Protect from Magic can be used against Karamel to minimise her stat drains.

  • Corrupt Lizardman
  • Elvarg
  • Glod

The following bosses have long kill times and are recommended to be disabled unless you bring a Magic switch:

  • Ice Troll King

This method utilises disabling Dream Mentor and Desert Treasure bosses due to a mechanic, where if Dream Mentor is enabled but Desert Treasure is not, point rewards are reduced to one sixth of their original value.[1] To maximise AFK, disabling Desert Treasure and Recipe for Disaster bosses is preferable due to the bosses either being click-intensive or requiring attention, and disable Dream Mentor to avoid the point penalty.

Gear[edit | edit source]

The gear recommended for this method in order of best to worst is on other parts of the strategy page: Dharok, Obsidian, highest strength gear, or mixed Strength bonus and Prayer gear.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Boost with an overload, consume 1000 points of absorption points, and feel the Locator orb or guzzle the dwarven rock cake down to 1 Hitpoints. Quickly turn Rapid Heal on and off to reset Hitpoints regeneration and do so every 50-60 seconds to maintain 1 Hitpoints. Make sure Auto Retaliate is on.

Follow the boss kill priority list provided to maximise points gained.

Never use Ultimate Force power-up. Always use the Power Surge power-up and use the special attack weapon constantly. Use Recurrent Damage and Zapper whenever for higher points per hour.

Bosses[edit | edit source]

Make sure the following bosses are disabled:

  • Recipe for Disaster and Desert Treasure bosses (due to non-AFK mechanics)
    • Dream Mentor bosses (due to point penalty if Desert Treasure is off)
  • Corrupt Lizardman (deals poison through absorption potions)
  • Elvarg (drains stats and prayer points)
  • Chronozon (requires casting 4 different magic spells)
  • Dagannoth mother (requires actively switching attack style)
  • Barrelchest (drains prayer)
  • Glod (drains prayer)
  • Witch's experiment (4-phase boss with low point reward)
  • Nazastarool (3-phase boss)
  • Slagilith (66% reduced damage for non-pickaxes)
  • Tanglefoot (Requires secateurs to damage)

Optional disables that will make it more AFK, but less points per hour:

  • Giant Roc (due to stun and knock back)
  • Dad (due to stun and knock back)
  • Essyllt (due to stat drain and a short stun)
  • Treus Dayth (moves around after damage)
